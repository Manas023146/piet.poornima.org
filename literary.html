About

It is associated with literary works and other formal writings. Students will get a floor to advertise their skills in writing and speaking. This club conducts activities regarding poetry, storytelling, extempore, debates, MUN etc.

Objectives

  • To help to prepare students to work in a community by teaching them about social, political and moral issues.
  • Making students be able to express themselves clearly
  • To help them groom their personalities so that they feel confident about themselves.
  • o eradicate any fear lingering in one's mind regarding proficiency in any language.

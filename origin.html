Introduction

Students are given a platform to show their potential in Creative Fields like Graphic Designing, Art & Craft,Audio and Video editing etc. Various technical and non-technical events will enhance the skills of students. Learning sessions will provide students a better platform to learn something new excluding academics which are important for future aspects of their career and lots more.

Objectives

To fulfill the thirst of the corporate world by updating the students' knowledge on recent trends and technologies in the Creative world.

Digital Library

The Digital Library at PIET is a hub of knowledge, offering a diverse range of resources accessible to students, faculty, and researchers.

Users have access to a plethora of online databases such as IEEE, J-Gate, DELNET, and e-Journals, as well as resources from the National Digital Library of India and e-kumbh, a government initiative.

The Digital Section of the library boasts computer systems, enabling users to explore e-books, e-journals, conference proceedings, reports, e-newspapers, and e-magazines to meet their academic needs.

Users benefit from both on-campus and remote access to resources, ensuring flexibility and convenience. Moreover, the library offers a Journal Content Service, wherein faculty members receive routed contents of recently received journals, facilitating access to relevant literature.

Through its robust digital infrastructure and comprehensive services, the Digital Library at PIET stands as a beacon of academic excellence, fostering research, learning, and scholarly engagement within the institution and beyond.

About

This club includes all the sports activities organised at COLLEGE level, jaipur level as well as state level. This club organizes all the inter college sports in various games like cricket, football, volleyball ball, carom, chess, table tennis and body building.

This club give an opportunity to the students to enhance their sporting skill sans showcase their talents on the field, helps the student not only in learning various technique but also develop sports man spirit.

Objectives

  • Sports club gives opportunity to go at different levels of competition, so our main objective is to make our team strong enough to achieve their goals and make our college proud
  • Other than that to build our team strong the ability of right understanding, teamwork and hard work
  • Also how to respect each other coaches and parents.

About

Gaming & Development Club is a society that organizes seminars and workshops on the new trends of technology in the field of online gaming and provides hands-on experience to the students. It also collaborates with eminent speakers and technocrats for providing a greater reach in the field of technology plus the experience of the industry.

Other than this the club also organizes hackathons and technical fests which showcases and help in the growth of emerging technologies.

Objectives

  • The main role of the club is to impart knowledge about various technologies.
  • The Gaming & Development Club provides a collaborative experience for like-minded individuals to work together, discover interests, and develop innovative games using new technologies.
  • It refers to a person's inherent capacity to study or perform in a certain subject.
  • Provide members with the training, tools, and guidance to complete their projects.

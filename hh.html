About

Helping Hands, as the name suggests, lifting up with hands for helping the society to stand together and spreading happiness. Club has successfully done many events such as charity in Government schools, Shared Birthday Project, volunteering in Jaipur Marathon, Blood Donation Camp, providing donations when needed, and many other noted events, also collaborating with other organizations whenever there is need.

Objectives

  • To build responsiveness among students about the social responsibilities.
  • Raising awareness about women rights.
  • To serve the society in its possible need and perform the responsibilities to the society.

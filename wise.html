About

This club is about awareness of Women in Science and Engineering. In today's scenario no one pays much attention towards their health, especially women. So to make women understand about their safety and health issues this club is originated.

Objectives

  • To organize entertaining & educational activities for development of women.

  • It refers to a person's inherent capacity to study or perform in a certain subject.
